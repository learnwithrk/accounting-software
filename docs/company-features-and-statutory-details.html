3 Company Features and Statutory Details

After filling the basic company details, press F11: Features or F3: Statutory & Taxation to configure additional options.

3.1 (a) Enable Statutory & Taxation

Set the following options to Yes as applicable:

Option Purpose
Enable Goods & Services Tax (GST) For GST-registered businesses.
Enable Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) If applicable under the Income Tax Act.
Enable Tax Collected at Source (TCS) For applicable transactions.

When prompted, enter GST details:

Field Example Entry
GSTIN 33ABCDE1234F1Z5
Applicable from 01-04-2024
Type of Registration Regular / Composition
Assessee Type Individual / Company / Partnership

3.4 5. Selection, Closure, and Alteration of Company

3.4.1 (a) Selecting a Company

  1. From the Gateway of Tally, select Select Company.
  2. Choose the desired company name from the list.

3.4.2 (b) Altering Company Information

  1. From the Company Info screen, select Alter.
  2. Modify details such as address, email, or GSTIN.
  3. Press Ctrl + A to save.

3.4.3 (c) Closing a Company

  1. Press Alt + F1 (Shut Company) to close the current company.
  2. The system will return to the Company Info menu.
